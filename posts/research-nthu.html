Summer Research at National Tsinghua University

暑期在台湾清华大学的研究经历

Posted on August 30, 2025 发布于 2025年8月30日

As part of the "Ta-You Wu Scholars Cross-Strait Summer Research Program," I spent two months at the Institute of Statistics and Data Science at NTHU.

作为“吴大猷学者两岸暑期研究项目”的一部分，我在台湾清华大学的统计与数据科学研究所度过了两个月。。

I learned a lot about data preprocessing, sentiment analysis models, and topic modeling. The academic environment was very stimulating, and I had great discussions with professors and fellow students. This experience solidified my interest in pursuing a research-oriented career.

我学到了很多关于数据预处理、情感分析模型和主题建模的知识。学术氛围非常激励人，我与教授和同学们进行了深入的讨论。这段经历坚定了我追求研究型职业的兴趣。

My Experience as a TA for Computer Programming

我的计算机程序设计助教经历

Posted on September 23, 2025 发布于 2025年9月23日

During the fall semester of 2025, I had the privilege of serving as a Teaching Assistant for the "Computer Programming L" course at USTC. This experience was incredibly rewarding, allowing me to solidify my own understanding of fundamental concepts while helping fellow students navigate their initial journey into programming.

在2025年秋季学期，我有幸担任了中国科学技术大学“计算机程序设计L”课程的助教。这段经历非常有意义，它让我在帮助同学们开启编程之旅的同时，也巩固了自己对基础概念的理解。

One of the biggest challenges was explaining complex concepts like pointers and recursion in a way that was easy for beginners to understand. It forced me to revisit these topics and solidify my own understanding. I found that using analogies and drawing diagrams was incredibly effective. Seeing the "aha!" moment on a student's face was the best reward.

最大的挑战之一是如何用初学者容易理解的方式解释像指针和递归这样的复杂概念。这迫使我重新审视这些主题，并巩固了自己的理解。我发现使用类比和画图非常有帮助。看到学生脸上露出“原来如此”的表情是最好的回报。

The best way to learn is to teach. This role was a testament to that saying.
教学相长。这个角色是对这句格言的最好证明。
